M’sotho-M’swati Trading is a Black Economic Empowerment (BEE) Company with an objective of youth empowerment and the community at large, by focusing on those problem areas where we feel we can use our skills constructively to make a difference to society and to become a role model for others. M’sotho-M’swati Trading is different from conventional business as we will also focus on community development. The business is based in Nelspruit, Mpumalanga Province. The business trades within and outside the commercial area of Mpumalanga Province to the other Provinces of South Africa.

About Us

M’sotho-M’swati Trading is committed to providing innovative, optimal, efficient and cost-effective services to meet the exact needs of their clients whilst maintaining high ethical professional standards. Our mission is to contribute meaningfully to socio-economic advancement of all communities we serve by ensuring a holistic and integrated solution towards the implantation of each project. Together we are committed to empower communities to play a significant role in development activities, which affect them directly. Our vision and mission is to be at the forefront of procurement and distribution. Our focus will be to provide consistence service excellence to our clients through personal caring approach, and by keeping abreast with the latest technology for the provision of services and products.
